Guitar Theory

The Application of Music theory to the Acoustic and Electric Guitar

Welcome to the basics of guitar theory, where we will introduce the necessities of music theory as it applies to the electric and acoustic guitar. Some of the basic concepts that will be covered include reading and writing tabalature, the notes on the fretboard, basic scales such as the natural major and minor, major and minor pentatonic, and harmonic minor scales, as well as many other topics.
